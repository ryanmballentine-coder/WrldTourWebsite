Paris, France

View Full Travel Guide

Currency: Euro (€)

Language: French

Population: ~2.1 million

Safety: High, but watch for pickpocketing

Climate: Mild year-round, best in Spring/Fall

Cost: $$$

Transport: Metro, buses, walking

Top Attractions:

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Louvre Museum
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral
  • Montmartre

Food & Culture:

  • Croissants, baguettes, fine dining
  • Wine and café culture
  • Fashion & art scene

Best Time: April–June, Sept–Oct

Tips:

  • Learn basic French phrases
  • Use metro passes
  • Avoid peak tourist hours
